About

Laybr is the platform to use when you have a task that needs to be done,
and need to find someone to do it. Laybr allows you to find someone to get this task done, in an intuitive and easy to use way. Our focus is on three main categories: Tutoring, Fitness, and Physical Labor. Tasks can be posted in these three categories and can be fulfilled by anyone.
